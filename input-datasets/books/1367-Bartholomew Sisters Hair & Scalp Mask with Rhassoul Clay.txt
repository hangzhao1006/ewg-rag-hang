Bartholomew Sisters Hair & Scalp Mask with Rhassoul Clay

Brand: Bartholomew Sisters

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Rhassoul clay, activated bamboo charcoal powder and neem powder.

Directions: Place all the ingredients minus the water in a bowl. Next, slowly add water and stir until you get a smooth paste (more liquidy than thick, so it s easy to apply).

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
