Root Science, Artic-C , Vitamin C Brightening Serum

Brand: Root Science

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil*, Squalane, Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate (Vitamin C), Prunus Domestica (Plum) Seed Oil*, Rubus Idaeus (Raspberry) Seed Oil*, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Oil*, Ribes Nigrum (Black Currant) Seed Oil*, Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn) Seed Oil*, Astaxanthin**, Bisabolol**, Tocopherol

Directions: Can be used daily AM or PM. We recommend using Arctic-C as an evening treatment to begin with, applying immediately after cleansing and toning. Apply 1-2 pumps of serum to damp face, neck and chest. Allow several minutes for serum to fully absorb. Additional moisturizer can be used after application, if desired. For those with sensitive skin, we recommend applying every second or third day (one or two days rest between application).
