Be Green Bath & Body Makeup Remover

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Makeup Remover

Ingredients: Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Europaea (Olive) Oil*, Ricinus Communis (Castor) Oil*, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil*, Citrus Nobilis (Mandarin Orange) Peel Oil.

Directions: Place a small amount on a cotton ball. Rub closed eyes, lips and face gently to remove makeup.
