Kaike Restorative Clay Powder Facial Mask + Scrub, Chocolate (2020 formulation)

Brand: Kaike

Category: Exfoliant/Scrub

Ingredients: : rhassoul clay, cacao powder, vanilla bean powder

Directions: As a mask: Mix 1 tbsp. with a few drops of water (or other liquid, such as aloe vera juice, milk, or honey, according to skincare need) to form a paste. Apply a thin layer to skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave on for 5-10 minutes then rinse off. Use as needed as a spot treatment, or weekly as a part of your skincare routine.
 
As a scrub: use as a cleansing grain by mixing a small amount of water or your facial cleanser of choice to 1 tsp. of powder. Form a paste and massage into skin then rinse.

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
