All Things Jill Facial Bar, Frankincense + Geranium

Brand: All Things Jill

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Aqua, Sodium cocoate (Coconut oil), Sodium olivate (Olive oil), Sodium ricate (Rice Bran oil), Sodium Shea butterate (Shea butter), Sodium avocadate (Avocado oil), Lavandula angustifolia (Lavender), Frankincense carterii (Frankincense), Pelargonium roseum (Geranium), Kaolinite (white clay), Illite (French pink clay)

Directions: Moisten soap with water. Lather in hands before applying to face and massaging in gentle circles. Rinse with clean water and follow up with toner and serum or moisturizer! May be used all over the body as well.

Ingredient concerns: HIGH: Allergies & Immunotoxicity; LOW: Cancer, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
