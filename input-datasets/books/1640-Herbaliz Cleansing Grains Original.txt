Herbaliz Cleansing Grains Original

Brand: Herbaliz

Category: Exfoliant/Scrub

Ingredients: Kaolin, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel*, Prunus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Meal*, Zea Mays (Corn) Meal*, Rose Hips*, Santalum Album (Sandalwood), Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel, Juglans Regia (Persian Walnut) Shell Powder, Foeniculum Vulgare (Fennel) Seeds*, Rose Petals*, Melissa Officinalis (Lemon Balm) Leaf Powder*. *Certified organic.

Directions: Pat grains with damp fingers then apply to face. Or pour a small amount into palm; then pat with damp fingers and gently massage into face, neck and decollete. Rinse. Use within 1 year of purchase.

Warnings: Allergy Alert: Includes almonds and oats. Not for internal use. Use within 1 year of purchase.
