Honest Beauty Magic Gel-to-Milk Cleanser

Brand: Honest Beauty

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Glycerin, Water (Aqua), Sucrose Laurate, Rosa Damascena Flower Water, Caprylyl Glycol, Sucrose Stearate, Sucrose Palmitate, Kaolin, Potassium Sorbate, Sodium Benzoate, Citric Acid
