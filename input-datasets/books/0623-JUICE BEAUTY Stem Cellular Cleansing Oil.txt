JUICE BEAUTY Stem Cellular Cleansing Oil

Brand: Juice Beauty

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Helianthus annuus (organic sunflower) seed oil, caprylic/capric triglyceride, polyglyceryl-4 isostearate, vitis vinifera (organic grape) seed oil, tetrahexyldecyl ascorbate (Vitamin C), tocopherol (Vitamin E), pyrus malus (organic apple) juice, citrus medica limonum (organic lemon) juice, malus domestica (apple) fruit cell culture extract, vitis vinifera (grape) fruit cell extract, citrus medica limonum (lemon) leaf cell extract, litsea cubeba (may chang) oil, cinnamomum camphora (ho wood) oil

Directions: For a softening, purifying cleanse, pump this lightweight cleansing oil on dry hands and massage into dry skin. Wet fingertips and continue to massage in circular motions to form a light emulsion. Then rinse with warm water. Pat dry.
For a spa-like cleanse, pump oil directly onto a warm damp washcloth. Gently massage in a circular motion, then rinse well with warm water. Finish by covering face with a warm, damp washcloth. Pat dry.
To remove eye makeup, pump a small amount onto a moist cloth and gently wipe makeup away.

Warnings: Do not use on broken or irritated skin. Discontinue if irritation
occurs. Avoid contact with the eyes. For external use only.
