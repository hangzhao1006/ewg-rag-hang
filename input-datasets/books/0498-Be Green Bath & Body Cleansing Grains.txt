Be Green Bath & Body Cleansing Grains

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Kaolin, Sativa (Oat) Kernel Flour, Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel Meal, Lavandula Officinalis (Lavender) Flower Powder*, Rosa Centifolia (Rose) Flower Powder*.

Directions: Place 1/2 tsp in hand or small bowl. Add 1/4 tsp water and mix to form paste. Massage skin gently. Rinse off or let dry to form mask then rinse off.
