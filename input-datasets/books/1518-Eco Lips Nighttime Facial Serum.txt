Eco Lips Nighttime Facial Serum

Brand: Eco Lips

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: Squalane, *Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Vaccinium Corymbosum (Blueberry) Seed Oil, *Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Bakuchiol, Mixed Tocopherols (Vitamin E), *Tanacetum Annuum (Blue Tansy) Oil.

Directions: Apply 2-3 drops to clean skin or add to your favorite facial moisturizer. Massage into face & decolletage every evening. Can be applied under eye area, avoiding the eye itself. Start with 1-2 times a week, gradually increasing to nightly use or as often as needed. Shake well before use.

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
