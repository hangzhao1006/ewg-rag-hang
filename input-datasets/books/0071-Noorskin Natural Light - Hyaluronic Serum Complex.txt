Noorskin Natural Light - Hyaluronic Serum Complex

Brand: Noorskin

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: water, propanediol, niacinamide, sodium hyaluronate, 1,2-hexanediol, panthenol, citric acid, caprylhydroxamic acid.

Directions: Use everyday on face, neck and decolletage. Apply a small amount gently on clean skin, starting at the center and spreading outwards. On forehead, cheeks and chin, use upwards movements; on the neck, use downwards movements.

Warnings: For external use only. Keep out of children's reach. Store in a dark, dry and ventilated place. In case of irritation, discontinue use and consult a doctor. This product has been formulated to minimize any possible allergic reactions
