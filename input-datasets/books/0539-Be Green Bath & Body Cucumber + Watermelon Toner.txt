Be Green Bath & Body Cucumber + Watermelon Toner

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Toners/Astringents

Ingredients: Hamamelis Virginiana (Witch Hazel) Hydrosol Water, Aqua (Distilled Water), Glycerin, Citrullus Lanatus (Watermelon) Fruit Extract, Cucumis Sativus (Cucumber) Fruit Extract, Camellia Sinensis (Green Tea) Leaf Extract, Salix Alba (Willow) Bark Extract, Lactobacillus Ferment, Propanediol, Ethylhexyl Glycerin, Potassium Sorbate.
