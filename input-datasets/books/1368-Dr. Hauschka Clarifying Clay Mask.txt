Dr. Hauschka Clarifying Clay Mask

Brand: Dr. Hauschka

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Loess, Tropaeolum Majus Flower/Leaf/Stem Extract, Hamamelis Virginiana (Witch Hazel) Bark/Leaf Extract, Zea Mays (Corn) Starch.

Directions: Use once or twice each week. Blend approx. 2 heaping teaspoons of Clarifying Clay Mask with an equal amount of water to create a smooth paste. Smooth the mask onto the face, neck and décolleté, if desired. Avoid eye area. For normal, blemished and oily skin remove after approx. 10 minutes with plenty of warm water or a damp cosmetic sponge.

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
