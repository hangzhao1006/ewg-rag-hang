Be Green Bath & Body Night Cream for Dry/Normal Skin

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Aqua (Distilled Water), Olea Europaea (Olive) Oil, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Persea Gratissima (Avocado) Oil, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Rosa Canina (Rosehip) Oil, Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose) Oil, Lactobacillus Ferment, Cetyl Alcohol, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut )Oil, Maranta Arundinacea (Arrowroot) Powder, Niacinimide, Potassium Sorbate, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Magnesium Sulfate, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Phytate, Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid.

Directions: Using clean hands, scoop a pea-sized amount onto finger and gently massage into skin on face, neck and decollete. Allow 2 minutes to penetrate.
