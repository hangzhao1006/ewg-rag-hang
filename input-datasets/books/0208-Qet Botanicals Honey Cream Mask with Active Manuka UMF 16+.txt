Qet Botanicals Honey Cream Mask with Active Manuka UMF 16+

Brand: Qet Botanicals

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Leptospermum Scoparium (Manuka) Honey UMF 16+, Kaolin (White Clay), Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Cranberry) Seed Oil, Helianthus Annus (Sunflower) Seed Oil*, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Flower Oil. *Organic Ingredient

Directions: Using a clean, dry finger, retrieve a small amount and spread on to dampened face. Let the Manuka veil rest five to ten minutes and remove with a warm washcloth. Gentle enough to use every day as the perfect morning mask.

Warnings: If you have a negative reaction, discontinue use and consult with a health professional.
