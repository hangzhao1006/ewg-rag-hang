DIME Beauty Gentle Jelly Cleanser

Brand: DIME

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: water, decyl glucoside, coco glucoside, glycerin, glyceryl oleate, hydrolyzed wheat protein, xanthan gum, Citric Acid, Strawberry Oil, lycopene, tocopherol, phenylpropanol, ethylhexylglycerin

Directions: Use in the morning and at night as the first step in your skincare routine. Wet face and apply cleanser to fingertips. Using a circular motion, work the cleanser into a consistent lather over the face and neck. Rinse with warm water and gently pat skin dry. For best results, use daily.
