EPICUTIS Lipid Recovery Eye Mask

Brand: Epicutis

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Aqua/Water/Eau, Glycerin, Propanediol, Pentylene Glycol, Caffeine, Disodium Tetramethylhexadecenyl Succinoyl Cysteine, Acetylglutaminoyl Farnesylcysteine, Sodium Hyaluronate, Hydrolyzed Sodium Hyaluronate, PPG-13-Decyltetradeceth-24, Sodium Phytate, Citric Acid

Directions: INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:
This bio-cellulose mask is covered by two
separate sheets. Tear open pouch, remove mask
from tray. Remove protective white mesh layer
and apply under eyes. Adjust the mask to
contour, then remove the protective blue layer.
Smooth out the mask to remove any air bubbles.
Leave on for 10-15 minutes. Remove mask and
discard. Gently massage any remaining liquid
into the skin. Do not rinse.

Warnings: SINGLE USE MASK
FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY
AVOID DIRECT CONTACT WITH THE EYES
