NENA Clay Mask

Brand: NENA

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Sea Silt (Glacial Oceanic Clay), Propanediol, Hydroxyacetophenone, Chondrus Crispus (Carrageenan) Powder.

Directions: Shake well before use. After cleansing apply clay evenly over the entire face, avoid eyes and mouth. Leave until dried. Wash off completely with warm or cold water. Use as often as needed, as a full face mask or spot treatment.

Warnings: None
