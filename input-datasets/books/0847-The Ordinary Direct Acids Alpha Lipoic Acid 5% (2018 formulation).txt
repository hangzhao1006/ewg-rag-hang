The Ordinary Direct Acids Alpha Lipoic Acid 5% (2018 formulation)

Brand: The Ordinary

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: Propanediol, Thioctic Acid

Directions: Apply 2-3 drops over the entire face a maximum of 2-3 times per week.

Warnings: This treatment is highly concentrated and daily use is not recommended. Do not use on irritated or sensitive skin. Can be diluted with most oils per application to reduce strength.

Ingredient concerns: MODERATE: Use Restrictions; LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity
