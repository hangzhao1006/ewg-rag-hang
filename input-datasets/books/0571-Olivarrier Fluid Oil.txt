Olivarrier Fluid Oil

Brand: Olivarrier

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Squalane (Olive Squalane)

Directions: Apply 1-2 drops and rub it with both hands. Gently cover your face with your hands until obsorbed.

Warnings: 1. Stop using the product at the following symptoms, and consult with a dermatologist as the symptom would deteriorate if used continuously. A) Symptoms such as red spots, swelling, itching or irritation during the use B) The above symptoms on the applied part due to direct sunlight 2. Do not use the product on the part with a scar, eczema or dermatitis 3. Precautions at Storage & Handling A) Close the cover after use B) Keep out of reach of the children
