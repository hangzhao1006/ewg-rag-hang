Be Green Bath & Body Night Cream for Sensitive/Combination Skin

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Aqua (Distilled Water),, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose) Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Cetyl Alcohol, Lactobacillus Ferment, Maranta Arundinacea Powder (Arrowroot), Niacinimide, Potassium Sorbate, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Magnesium Sulfate, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Phytate, Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid

Directions: Using clean hands, scoop a pea-sized amount onto finger and gently massage into skin on face, neck and decollete. Allow 2 minutes to penetrate.
