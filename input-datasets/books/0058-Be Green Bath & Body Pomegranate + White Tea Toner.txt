Be Green Bath & Body Pomegranate + White Tea Toner

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Toners/Astringents

Ingredients: Rosa Damascena (Rose) Hydrosol Water, Aqua (Distilled Water), Oryza Sativa (Rice) Extract, Camellia Sinesis (White Tea) Leaf Extract, Punica Grantum (Pomegranate) Fruit Extract, Centella Asiatica (Gotu Kola) Extract, Lactobacillus, Euterpe Oleracea (Acai) Fruit Extract, Propanediol, Ethylhexyl Glycerin, Potassium Sorbate.

Directions: Cleanse skin before using. Moisten a cotton pad with a small amount of toner and apply to skin. For toners with a spray cap, CLOSE EYES and spritz 3-5 times on face. Follow with facial serum or moisturizer.
