Cliganic Organic Grapeseed Oil

Brand: Cliganic

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Vitis vinifera (grape) seed oil

Directions: Apply 2-3 drops of Cliganic Organic Grape Seed Oil into your hands. Rub together to warm before massaging onto a clean, dry face and neck, avoiding eye areas. Use morning and evening, and as needed. Cliganic Organic Grape Seed Oil can be layered under additional creams and oil based products for added moisture.

Warnings: For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes keep away from flames and high heat. Keep out of reach of children. If irritation or redness occurs discontinue immediately and consult with a doctor.
