Honest Beauty Gentle Gel Cleanser

Brand: Honest Beauty

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Water (Aqua/Eau), Sodium Laurylglucosides Hydroxypropylsulfonate, Sodium Methyl Cocoyl Taurate, Cocamidopropyl Hydroxysultaine, Glycerin, Sodium Chloride, Sorbitol, Sodium Lauryl Glucose Carboxylate, Lauryl Glucoside, Calendula Officinalis Flower Extract, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil, Lavandula Hybrida Oil, Pelargonium Graveolens Flower Oil, Pogostemon Cablin Leaf Oil, Anthemis Nobilis Flower Oil, Cymbopogon Martini Oil, Abies Sibirica Oil, Gluconolactone, Potassium Sorbate, Sodium Benzoate, Coconut Acid, Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid, Sorbic Acid, Sodium Methyl Taurate, Linalool, Linalyl Acetate.

Directions: Morning skin care routine (AM)

Step 1: Squeeze one pump of gel onto your fingertips

Step 2: Gently massage your bare face in a circular motion to remove impurities. (TIP: Start from the center of your face and work outward)

Step 3: Wet skin with water to rinse off the cleanser

Step 4: Dry face and apply moisturizer

Night skin care routine (PM)

Step 1: Squeeze one pump of gel onto your fingertips

Step 2: Gently massage your face to remove makeup

Step 3: Wet skin with regular or micellar water to rinse off the cleanser and makeup

Warnings: Use as directed. Avoid direct contact with eyes. If contact occurs, flush thoroughly with water.
