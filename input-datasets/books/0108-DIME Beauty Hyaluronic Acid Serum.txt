DIME Beauty Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Brand: DIME

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: Water, Sodium hyaluronate, phenoxyethanol, ethylhexylglycerin

Directions: After cleansing face with a DIME cleanser, apply 3-4 drops every morning and night (or as needed) to the face and neck area. Massage into skin. Make sure to follow with a DIME moisturizer. For best results, apply daily.
