Mario Badescu Cleansing Oil

Brand: Mario Badescu

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Cetyl Ethylhexanoate, Polyglyceryl-10 Dioleate, Isononyl Isononanoate, Polyglyceryl-10 Oleate, Pentaerythrityl Tetraethylhexanoate, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Oryza Sativa (Rice) Bran Oil, Polyglyceryl-2 Oleate, Phenoxyethanol, Aqua (Water, Eau), Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose) Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Chlorphenesin, Caprylyl Glycol, Lavandula Hybrida Oil

Directions: Apply 1-2 pumps on a cotton pad to remove makeup. As a cleanser, apply 1-2 pumps on dry face with fingertips. Add warm water and massage to emulsify. For use morning and night.

Warnings: If product gets into eyes, rinse well with water

Ingredient concerns: HIGH: Use Restrictions; MODERATE: Allergies & Immunotoxicity; LOW: Cancer, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity
