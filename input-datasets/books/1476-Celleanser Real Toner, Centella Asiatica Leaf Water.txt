Celleanser Real Toner, Centella Asiatica Leaf Water

Brand: Celleanser

Category: Toners/Astringents

Directions: After washing your face, take out an appropriate amount of toner and spread it evenly on your face to absorb it. - A proper amount for a cotton pad.
