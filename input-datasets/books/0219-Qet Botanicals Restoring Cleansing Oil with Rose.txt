Qet Botanicals Restoring Cleansing Oil with Rose

Brand: Qet Botanicals

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Olea Europaea (Olive) Fruit Oil*, Ricinus Communis (Castor) Bean Oil*, Rosa Damascena (Rose) Flower Oil, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Flower Oil*, Jasminum Officinale Sambac (Jasmine) Flower Oil, Matricaria Recutica (Chamomile) Flower Oil*, Pelargonium Graveolens (Geranium) Flower Oil*. *Organic Ingredient

Directions: Shake and place a small puddle in the palm of your hand. Massage into skin as much as necessary. Moisten washcloth with warm water and press on to your skin to remove, or add exfoliator before steaming away. Use every evening. There is usually no need to re-cleanse in the morning, just prepare skin for the serum.

Warnings: If you have a negative reaction, discontinue use and consult with a health professional.
