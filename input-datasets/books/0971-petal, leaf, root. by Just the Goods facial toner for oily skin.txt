petal, leaf, root. by Just the Goods facial toner for oily skin

Brand: petal, leaf, root.

Category: Toners/Astringents

Ingredients: lavandula angustifolia flower water, rosmarinus officinalis leaf oil, achillea millefolium oil

Directions: Shake well before use. Apply to a cotton disc, then wipe face in upward motions avoiding the eyes. Repeat as needed until no more oil/dirt from skin transfers to the cotton disc. Store in a cool, dark place.

Warnings: Avoid eyes

Ingredient concerns: MODERATE: Allergies & Immunotoxicity; LOW: Cancer, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
