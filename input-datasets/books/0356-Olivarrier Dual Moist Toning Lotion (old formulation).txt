Olivarrier Dual Moist Toning Lotion (old formulation)

Warnings: 1. Stop using the product at the following symptoms, and consult with a dermatologist as the symptom would deteriorate if used continuously. A) Symptoms such as red spots, swelling, itching or irritation during the use. B) The above symptoms on the applied part due to direct sunlight. 2. Do not use the product on the part with a scar, eczema or dermatitis. 3. Precautions at Storage & Handling. A) Close the cover after use. B) Keep out of reach of the children. C) Do not store in a place of high temperature, low temperature of under direct sunlight.
