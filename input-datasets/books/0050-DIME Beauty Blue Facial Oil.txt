DIME Beauty Blue Facial Oil

Brand: DIME

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel Oil, Squalane, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Limnanthes Alba (Meadowfoam) Seed Oil, Rosa Canina (Rose hip) Seed Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Silybum Marianum (Milk Thistle) Seed Oil, Nigella Sativa (Black Cumin) Seed Oil, Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil, Punica Granatum (Pomegranate) Seed Oil, Tanacetum Annuum (Blue Tansy) Flower Oil, Tocopherol

Directions: Dispense 3-4 drops of oil into the palm of your hand. Gently rub the oil between your hands and lightly pat and press onto the entire face, neck, or affected area. Apply in the evening after moisturizing. Blue Facial Oil can also be used as a spot treatment.
