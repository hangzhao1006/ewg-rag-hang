DEW MTY Bloom Jelly Serum Bar (2020 formulation)

Brand: DEW MTY

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Ethylhexyl Olivate, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil, Dibutyl Lauroyl Glutamide, Dibutyl Ethylhexanoyl Glutamide, Squalane, Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate, Gardenia Tahitensis Flower, Jasminum Officinale (Jasmine) Oil, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Oil, Hibiscus Sabdariffa (Hibiscus) Seed Oil, Magnolia Officinalis Bark Extract, Tanacetum Annuum Flower/Leaf/Stem Oil, Cananga Odorata Flower Oil, Stearyl Glycyrrhetinate, Silica.

Directions: Remove from shipping carton and place in the palm of your hand. Gently rub into your hands for distribution of product and use fingers and palms to transfer product pat where hydration and calming is needed. You can also cup the bar within your palm or hold between 2 fingers and slowly swipe directly onto the skin. Jelly Serum releases from its form when gently rubbed so you can decide how much is needed and where! Once completed place the bar within a permanent holder and carry throughout the day for touchups whenever a burst of moisture is needed.

Warnings: For external use only. Discontinue use if irritation develops.

Ingredient concerns: HIGH: Allergies & Immunotoxicity; LOW: Cancer, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
