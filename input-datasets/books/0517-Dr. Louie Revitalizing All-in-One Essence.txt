After washing your face with Mild Face Cleanser, apply a small amount of Revitalizing All-in-One Essence to your skin and gently pat into skin until fully absorbed.

Dab a small amount of Revitalizing All-in-One Eye Cream (optional) around your eye area, followed by Protection Cream (optional) for further protection against weather and elements.

Evening:
Dab a small amount of Revitalizing All-in-One Eye Cream (optional).

Warnings: :
Patch test first. Stop using if irritation occurs. For external use only. Keep reach out of children.
