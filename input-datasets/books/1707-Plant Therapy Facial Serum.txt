Plant Therapy Facial Serum

Brand: Plant Therapy

Category: Serums & Essences

Ingredients: Aqua (Water), Glycerin, 3-Glyceryl Ascorbate (Vitamin C), Sodium Hyaluronate, Ferulic Acid, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis (Orange) Peel Oil, Olive Oil Glycereth-8 Esters, Aloe Barbadensis (Aloe Vera) Leaf Juice, Phenoxyethanol, Xanthan Gum, Ethylhexylglycerin, Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid, Sodium Phytate
