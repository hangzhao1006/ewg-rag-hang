Inna Organic Botanical Trio Ultra-Soothing Face Mask

Brand: Inna Organic

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Aqua, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice*, Glycerin, Hordeum Vulgare Leaf Juice*, Propanediol, Sodium Levulinate, Betula Pendula Sap*, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Sodium Anisate, Sodium Dehydroacetate, Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate, Xanthan Gum, Olea Europaea (Olive) Leaf Extract, Citric Acid, Caulerpa Lentillifera Extract, Tremella Fuciformis Extract, Sodium Hyaluronate, Aster Maritima/Tripolium Extract, Tripleurospermum Maritimum Extract

Directions: 1. Take out the sheet mask from the package, unfold and gently place the sheet mask onto freshly cleansed face. 
2. Remove the white outer layer. 
3. Relax and remove the mask after 10-15 minutes. 
4. If any serum remains on the skin, tap lightly with fingertips until absorbed.

Warnings: If irritation occurs, discontinue use and consult a doctor if necessary. For external use only. Keep out of reach from children. 
Cautions:
1. Avoid direct contact with eyes. Rinse thoroughly if contact occurs. 
2. The product color may slightly vary from yellow to brown due to the use of Sodium Dehydroacetate, a Cosmos-approved preservative system. It is a normal condition that does not affect the quality of the product. 
Storage: Store in a cool and dry place, away from exposure to direct sunlight.
