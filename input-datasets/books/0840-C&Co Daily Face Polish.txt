C&Co Daily Face Polish

Brand: C & Co.

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Honey*, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Oil*, Sodium Bicarbonate**, Rosa Canina (Rosehip) Seed Oil*, Lavandula Officinalis (Lavender) Oil, Trehalose

Directions: Stir with spoon until thick in consistency and amber in color. Apply desired amount to wet skin, massage in a circular motion. Rinse and pat dry. Please avoid the eyes.
