COSRX Centella Cleansing Powder

Brand: COSRX

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Zea Mays (Corn) Starch, Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Sodium Lauroyl Glutamate, Sodium Lauroyl Aspartate, Maltodextrin, Allantoin, Centella Asiatica Extract, Lipase, Protease

Directions: Use morning and night,after removing makeup. Begin with wet hands and face. Pour a pack of powder into hands and rub together with 3-4 drops of water to create a creamy foam. Gently massage onto face avoiding the eye and mouth area. Rinse with warm water.

Ingredient concerns: HIGH: Allergies & Immunotoxicity; LOW: Cancer, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
