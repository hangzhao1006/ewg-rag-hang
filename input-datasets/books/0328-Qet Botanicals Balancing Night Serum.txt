Qet Botanicals Balancing Night Serum

Brand: Qet Botanicals

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil*, Helianthus Annus (Sunflower) Seed Oil*, Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose) Seed Oil*, Glycerine (Vegetable)*, Argania Spinosa (Argan) Fruit Oil*, Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Cranberry) Seed Oil, Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn) Fruit Oil*, Tocopherol (Non-GMO Vitamin E), Jasminum Officinale Grandiflorum (Jasmine) Flower Oil, Boswellia Carterii (Frankincense) Oil. *Organic Ingredient

Directions: Shake well and place three to five drops of serum in your palm. Blend with two mists of your favorite Toning Water to aid in the spreading and absorption of the serum. Press gently on to face and neck. Balances while at rest.

Warnings: If you have a negative reaction, discontinue use and consult with a health professional.
