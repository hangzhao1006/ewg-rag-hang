Inna Organic Green Tea Rose Hydrosol Calming Face Mask

Brand: Inna Organic

Category: Mask

Ingredients: Aqua, Rosa Damascena Flower Water, Glycerin, Propanediol, Aloe Barbadensis leaf juice, Hordeum Vulgare Leaf Juice, Betaine, Sodium Levulinate, Avena Sativa (Oat Kernel Flour), Sodium Anisate, Oryza sativa Extract, Olea Europaea (Olive) Leaf Extract, Sodium Dehydroacetate, Dipotassium Glycyrrhizate, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Hyaluronate, Citric Acid

Directions: 1. Take out the sheet mask from the package, unfold and gently place the sheet mask onto freshly cleansed face.
2. Remove the white outer layer.
3. Relax and remove the mask after 10-15 minutes.
4. If any serum remains on the skin, tap lightly with fingertips until absorbed.

Warnings: 1. If irritation occurs, discontinue use and consult a doctor if necessary. 
2. For external use only. Keep out of reach from children. 
3. The product color may slightly vary from yellow to brown due to the use of Sodium Dehydroacetate, a Cosmos-approved preservative system. It is a normal condition that does not affect the quality of the product.
