YOKEN ROSEHIP SEED OIL

Brand: YOKEN

Category: Body Oil

Ingredients: ROSA CANINA (ROSEHIP) SEED OIL

Directions: APPLICATIONS
FOR SKIN: Apply an appropriate amount to your skin and gently massage to be absorbed, to reach a good result, use twice daily and 8 weeks continuously.
FOR HAIR: Apply 6-8 drops gently massage on hair, split ends, or massage into dry scalp and roots.Can be used on damp or dry hair for daily hair care.

Warnings: Keep out of reach of children. For external use only. Avoid direct contact with eyes. Please do a skin test before use, if you encounter any problems during use, please consult your physician.
