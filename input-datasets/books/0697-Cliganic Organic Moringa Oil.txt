Cliganic Organic Moringa Oil

Brand: Cliganic

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: 100% moringa oleifera (moringa) oil

Directions: Apply 2-3 drops of Cliganic Organic Moringa Oil onto hands. Rub together to warm before massaging onto a clean, dry face and neck, avoiding eye areas. Use morning and evening, and as needed. Cliganic Organic Moringa oil can be layered under additional creams and oil-based products for added moisture.

Warnings: For external use only. Keep away from flames and high heat. Keep out of reach of children. Discontinue use immediately if redness or irritation occurs.
