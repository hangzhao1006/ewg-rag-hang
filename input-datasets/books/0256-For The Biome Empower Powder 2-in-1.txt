For The Biome Empower Powder 2-in-1

Brand: For The Biome

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Aloe Barbadensis (Aloe) Leaf*, Avena Sativa (Sprouted Oat) Sprout*, Black Strap Molasses*, Rice Protein*, Ganoderma Lucidum (Reishi)*, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower*, Calendula Officinalis (Marigold) Flower*, Emblica Officinalis (Amla) Fruit*, Cinnamomum Verum (Cinnamon) Bark*, Eugenia Caryophyllus (Clove) Bud*, Crocus Sativus (Saffron) Flower*, Withania Somnifera (Ashwaghanda) Root*, Curcuma Longa (Turmeric) Rhizome*, Saccharomyces cerevisiae*, Lactobacillus reuteri, Lactobacillus rhamnosus, Lactobacillus casei, Lactobacillus plantarum, Bifidobacterium longum

Directions: USE AS CLEANSER Mix up to ½ teaspoon of powder with equal parts warm water. Using fingertips, massage evenly over damp skin with gentle circular motions for 15-30 seconds. Rinse thoroughly with warm water. 

USE AS MASK Mix up to ½ teaspoon of powder with equal parts warm water. Using fingertips, apply a thin layer evenly over fresh, damp skin. Let sit until dry or for up to 10 minutes. Remoisten mask, massage, and rinse well. Indulge 1-2 times a week or more. 
If desired, follow with Restore Essence Spray for complete daytime protection.
