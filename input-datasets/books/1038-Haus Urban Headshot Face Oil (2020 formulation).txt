Haus Urban Headshot Face Oil (2020 formulation)

Brand: Haus Urban

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Organic Vitis vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Raphanus Sativus (Daikon Radish) Seed Oil, Sclerocarya Birrea (Marula) Seed Oil, Organic Camellia Oleifera (Camellia) Oil, Organic Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil

Directions: After cleansing, toning, and applying serum, apply 3-5 drops of Headshot Hydrating Face Oil to the hand, rub together to activate and pat gently into the face (paying special attention to any dry areas). For daytime use, follow up with Curtain Call Clarifying Moisturizer to seal and sunscreen to protect. For nighttime use, apply as the final step in your routine before bed.

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
