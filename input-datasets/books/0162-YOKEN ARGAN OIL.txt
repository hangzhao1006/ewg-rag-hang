YOKEN ARGAN OIL

Brand: YOKEN

Category: Body Oil

Ingredients: ARGANIA SPINOSA (ARGAN) OIL

Directions: APPLICATIONS
FOR HAlR 
Apply 6-8 drops gently massage on hair, split ends, or massage into dry scalp and roots. Can be used on damp or dry hair for daily hair care.
FOR SKIN 
Apply an appropriate amount to your skin after showering to hydrate and seal in moisture, and gently massage to be absorbed.
FOR NAIL
Apply to top & underside of nails, and massage into surrounding skin.

Warnings: Keep out of reach of children. For external use only. Avoid direct contact with eyes. Please do askin test before use, if you encounter any problems during use, please consult your physician.
