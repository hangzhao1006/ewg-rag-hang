Qet Botanicals Balancing Day Serum

Brand: Qet Botanicals

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Carthamus Tinctorius (Safflower) Seed Oil*, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil*, Oenothera Biennis (Evening Primrose) Seed Oil*, Glycerine (Vegetable)*, Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Cranberry) Seed Oil, Brassica Oleracea (Broccoli) Seed Oil, Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn) Fruit Oil*, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Flower Oil*, Tocopherol (Non-GMO Vitamin E), Jasminum Sambac (Jasmine) Flower Oil, Rubus Idaeus (Red Raspberry) Seed Oil, Daucus Carota (Carrot) Seed Oil*. *Organic Ingredient

Directions: Shake well and place three to five drops of serum in your palm. Blend with two mists of your favorite Toning Water to aid in the spreading and absorption of the serum. Press gently on to face, neck, and any exposed skin for natural protection.

Warnings: If you have a negative reaction, discontinue use and consult with a health professional.
