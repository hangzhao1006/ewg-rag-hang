The Spa Dr. CLEAR: Gentle Cleanser (Step 1)

Brand: The Spa Dr.

Category: Facial Cleanser

Ingredients: Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil*, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Almond) Oil, Arnica Montana (Arnica) Flower Extract*, Aqua (Water), Aloe Barbadensis (Aloe) Leaf Juice Powder*, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil*, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil*, Glycerin, Sucrose Laurate, Glyceryl Behenate.

Directions: For best results use morning and evening. Apply pea size amount to damp skin and gently massage with fingertips over face, neck and eye area. Use more if removing make up. Rinse and remove cleanser with a warm damp face cloth. (Tip: You can make the cleanser thinner by mixing a small amount of water with the cleanser in the palm of your hand or thicker by applying the cleanser without water.)
