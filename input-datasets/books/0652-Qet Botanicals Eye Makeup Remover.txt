Qet Botanicals Eye Makeup Remover

Brand: Qet Botanicals

Category: Makeup Remover

Ingredients: Carthamus Tinctorius (Safflower) Seed Oil*, Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil*, Vaccinium Macrocarpon (Cranberry) Seed Oil, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Flower Oil*, Tocopherol (Non-GMO Vitamin E). *Organic Ingredient

Directions: Tip bottle on to fingertip and gently massage eyelids and lashes. Avoid getting into eyes. Use warm washcloth for removal. Gently removes the day's eye makeup leaving the skin feeling supple and your lashes enriched. Use sparingly.

Warnings: If you have a negative reaction, discontinue use and consult with a health professional.
