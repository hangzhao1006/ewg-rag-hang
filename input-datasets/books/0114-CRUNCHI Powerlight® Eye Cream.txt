Directions: For Best Results: 
Using your clean ring finger, gently tap a dime-sized amount of Powerlight™ onto the skin around your entire eye area morning and night. 


Pro Tips: 
Store Powerlight® in the refrigerator for even more under-eye magic. 

To use as a quick and effective eye mask, place a heavier layer (about a quarter size amount) of Powerlight® under the eye area. Leave for 5-10 minutes and press any remaining product into the skin.
