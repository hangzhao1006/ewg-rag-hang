ZealSea Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Brand: ZealSea

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Aqua, Butylene glycol, Glycereth-26, Trehalose, Pentylene glycol, Sodium hyaluronate, 1,2-hexanediol, Allantoin, Hydroxyacetophenone, Ethylhexylglycerin, Panthenol, Dipotassium glycyrrhizate, Xylitylglucoside, Anhydroxylitol, Xylitol, Sodium acetylated hyaluronate, Sodium hyaluronate crosspolymer, Glucose, Hydrolyzed sodium hyaluronate.

Directions: Every morning and evening, apply 2-3 drops of serum to thoroughly cleansed skin and massage from the inside of your face working outwards, avoiding the eye area.

Warnings: 1. Store it in a dry and cool place, avoid the direct sun exposure.
2. If any discomfort or allergic symptom occurs, please discontinue using immediately and consult a doctor if necessary.
3. Keep out of children.
