Be Green Bath & Body Facial Serum for Sensitive Skin

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil*, Helianthus Annus (Sunflower) Seed Oil*, Tocopherol, Pogostemon Cablin (Patchouli) Oil*, Pelargonium Graveolens (Geranium) Oil*, Lavandula Angustifolia (Lavender) Oil.

Directions: Place 2-3 drops on finger and massage gently into skin on face and under eyes. Repeat with 3 drops for neck and chest. For best results use after toner and before moisturizer.
