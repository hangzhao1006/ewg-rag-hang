White Witch Gentle Exfoliating Cleanser (2018 formulation)

Brand: White Witch

Category: Exfoliant/Scrub

Ingredients: Avena Sativa Kernel Meal*, Lavanula Officinalis Flower Powder*, Calendula Officinalis Flower Powder*, Melissa Officinalis Leaf Powder*.

Directions: Place 2 scoops of powder into palm or smal container. Add 1 tsp. of water to make a paste. Apply to face and neck using fingertips. For best effect leave mask to dry for 5-10 mns. Rinse off. Gently pat face dry.
