Flower Mills Beauty Repairing Mask, Cacao

Brand: Flower Mill Beauty

Category: Mask

Ingredients: raw cacao*, white kaolin clay, lavender buds*, lavender essential oil*

Directions: Use ideally in the PM, no more than 2 times per week. In a small bowl, mix a nickel-sized amount of the powder with water until desired consistency is reached. Apply to face and neck, avoiding contact with eyes. Let dry, then remove with a warm, damp cloth. Follow up with your favorite moisturizer. If irritation occurs, cease use and contact a physician. Use only as directed on unbroken skin.

Ingredient concerns: LOW: Cancer, Allergies & Immunotoxicity, Developmental and Reproductive Toxicity, Use Restrictions
