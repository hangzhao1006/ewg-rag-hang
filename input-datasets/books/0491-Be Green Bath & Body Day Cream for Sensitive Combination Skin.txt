Be Green Bath & Body Day Cream for Sensitive/Combination Skin

Brand: Be Green Bath & Body

Category: Facial Moisturizer/Treatment

Ingredients: Aqua (Distilled Water), Vitis Vinifera (Grape) Seed Oil, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Zinc Oxide, Lactobacillus Ferment, Cetyl Alcohol, Maranta Arundincea (Arrowroot) Powder, Sodium Phytate, Potassium Sorbate, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Magnesium Sulfate, Sodium Citrate, Citric Acid.

Directions: Using clean hands, scoop a pea-sized amount onto finger and gently massage into skin on face, neck and decollete. Allow 2 minutes to penetrate.
